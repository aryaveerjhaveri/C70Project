pre school

established in 2005

An initiative by Inventure Academy, the Inventure PreSchool located in the heart of Whitefield is a bright, airy and thoughtfully designed learning space that caters to our youngest learners in the ages 2 years to 6 years. A verdant landscape of century old trees and large open spaces, our happy space focuses on the benefits of this green environment which enhances physical fitness combined with access to safe, natural areas improves overall health even among PreSchoolers. The programs and learning approaches mirror the environment provided at the main campus, with the advantage of shorter commute time for families in Whitefield and assured seats at Inventure PreSchool in Grade 1.
